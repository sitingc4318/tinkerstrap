What is Hot Pot?

Hotpots is a type of food that come from the Western Side. It is not only delicious but also gives you a great experience because there is different soup bases you can choose. Such as herbal, bone, tomato, and kimchi. Futhermore, you will need to put different varieties of foods that you like and boil it in the soup you choose. There are vegetabes, meats, seafoods, and noodles that can be put in. In addition, to add extra flavors you can dip the food into the sauces that you make.
